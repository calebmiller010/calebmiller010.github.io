## The Problem

A plumber's van sitting in a repair shop for a day costs thousands in lost revenue. Traditional fleet maintenance requires driving vehicles to a shop, waiting days for service, and losing billable hours.

## The Model

KC Fleet Solutions sends certified mobile mechanics directly to a business's parking lot — after hours or on weekends — for routine maintenance (oil changes, brakes, tires). The result is a pure ROI sell: clients never lose a working day.

## What I Built

- Full B2B commercial website and operations foundation
- Modeled on drop-service remote operations architecture
- Designed for the Kansas City metro commercial vehicle market (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, delivery)

## What's Next

A client dashboard for fleet management is in development — a centralized place for fleet operators to view and manage all their vehicles, maintenance schedules, and service history in one place.
